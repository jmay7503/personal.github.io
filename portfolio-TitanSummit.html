Titan Summit is a REMI production where our host, Sebastian Vang, talks with fellow members of the UW Oshkosh Model UN Team while they are at a competition in New York City. We used vMix to take the signal from NYC back to our control room in Oshkosh, WI, where we directed the broadcast.
